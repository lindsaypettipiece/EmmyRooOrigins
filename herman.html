Herman Metcalf

Herman Metcalf was your great grandfather. He was married to your great grandmother Mary. Herman grew up in Alabama. He had a little brother named Larry. Later in his life he joined the military and ended up in San Francisco, California. This is where he met the love of his life Mary Metcalf. They got married and had three children. Their first was your Grandma Kathleen. Then 2 years later came your Great Uncle Richard. And finally 2 years after that your Great Aunt Bonnie completed the Metcalf family!
